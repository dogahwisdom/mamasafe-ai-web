How to Delete Your Data

In compliance with Meta's User Data Control policy, MamaSafe AI provides immediate and direct ways for you to permanently delete your data from our systems. You can choose either of the following two paths to wipe your data:

Option 1: Path via WhatsApp Chat

The fastest way to delete your data is directly from the WhatsApp chat thread.

  • Open your MamaSafe AI chat in WhatsApp.
  • Reply with the keyword DELETE in the chat.
  • You will receive an automated confirmation that your data has been successfully wiped.

Option 2: Path via Email

You can request data deletion directly from our support team.

  • Send an email to support@mamasafeai.com.
  • Use the exact subject line: Data Deletion Request.
  • Please include the phone number associated with your WhatsApp chat to help us identify your record.
  • We will process your request within 24 hours and send you a confirmation.
